About

While the work for my volunteership in Sweden was drying up due to the COVID-19 pandemic, I took advantage of the extra time by learning D3. Almost simultaneously Sciensano published the data of the outbreak in Belgium as open data. So I made some interactive visualizations of the data on Observable.

They can be viewed here.
